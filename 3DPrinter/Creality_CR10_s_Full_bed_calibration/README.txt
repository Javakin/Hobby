# Summary

A no nonsense full bed calibration that will give you a clear picture of where your print adhesion problems lie. Print at 30mms for nice results.
Suitable for all beds with a maximum printable area of 300mmx300mm

Note: There is also a pre sliced version for PLA for those using a BL touch Bed Leveling Device.
