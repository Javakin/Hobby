# Summary

Big knob for the CR-10 bed adjustment.

These have an "up" indicator, relating the rotation direction to moving the bed upward, closer to the nozzle.  ...I can never remember which way to turn the knob.

The big detents are modeled after aperture ring adjusters on old manual lenses.

This all works awesome!!!  Super easy to roll the knob with one finger.
