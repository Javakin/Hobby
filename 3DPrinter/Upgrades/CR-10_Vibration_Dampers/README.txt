# Summary

Remix of the Anet A8 Vibration Dampening Feet found here: https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:2103227


These PLA vibration dampers help to significantly reduce printer noise, and in my personal experience have helped to noticeably eliminate ghosting/ringing in my prints. 

# Print Settings

Printer: Creality CR10
Rafts: No
Supports: Doesn't Matter
Resolution: .15-.2
Infill: 100%

Notes: 
These fit right onto the stock rubber feet. 

You will need qty: 4 of each part. 

PRINT THEM ON EDGE!

Pull the helpers into the dampers...don't push or you'll break them. 

Happy Printing :)
